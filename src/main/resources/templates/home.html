Registry

Get certified copies of registry records for the purpose of tracing family history and finding information about Your ancestors. Specifically, You can obtain birth, marriage, and death certificates for members of family who were born, married, or died.

Registry Requests

Work

If you have previously worked for a company that has archive material in our archives, we may be able to provide you with a work certificate to verify your employment history. A work certificate is a document that confirms your employment dates, job title, and other relevant details.

Work Requests

School

We can help you locate school certificates that have been archived in the state archives. Schools maintain registry books of student records that are eventually transferred to the archives for safekeeping. Contact us if you need help finding your school certificate.

Education Requests

Cadastre

Looking for information about a property or land parcel? Our archives hold cadastre records that can provide details about land ownership, boundaries, and other relevant information.

Cadastral Requests
